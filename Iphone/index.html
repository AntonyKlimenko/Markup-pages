WHAT'S NEW

All models are equipped with a single-chip A16 Bionic system, 48-megapixel main camera and ProMotion screens with Ceramic Shield protection and a frequency range of 1 - 120 Hz, as well as accelerated LPDDR5 memory. The design of the line has been updated, on the front panel of smartphones there are two cutout.

All iPhone 14s in the US will be sold without a SIM tray: the manufacturer says that improved eSIM technology will allow you to transfer old electronic SIM cards to new ones smartphones.

The front camera has been updated (it is equipped with autofocus and faster recognizes the user in low light conditions) and the main camera sensor.

The iPhone 14 Pro came out in a new design - without the branded “bangs”. Also in the range appeared new color. The smartphone, unlike the "regular" 14 iPhones, has an A16 processor Bionic, which can perform 4 trillion photo operations per second.
