Comfort and safety from HIMO

The HIMO C26 electric bike will allow you to quickly and comfortably get to your place of work or study and not be attached to public transport. The bike has a solid construction and impressive range to become a reliable vehicle in modern urban conditions. Lightweight and strong frame, 26" wheels and 7-speed system transmissions allow you to move confidently not only on an asphalt road canvas, but also on a difficult country road.

Strong and lightweight frame

The HIMO C26 frame is made of durable and lightweight aluminum alloy and features a high reliability, stability in emergency situations, excellent handling and comfortable travel on any road.

Combination of modes for extended range

The HIMO C26 electric bike has an extended range. Its battery capacity is 10000 mAh with intelligent energy optimization system allows you to drive up to 60 km on one charge in electric drive mode. When used on some sections of the road physical strength, the range increases to 100 km. Maximum movement speed HIMO C26 is 25 km/h.

Reliable transmission

7-speed transmission system provides smooth and precise shifting, convenient speed control and reliable operation. By changing gears, you can with minimal effort, drive up the hill and slide down safely.

All information on the LCD display

A large informative display, placed on the steering column, displays all the important information about the operation of HIMO C26 in real time. You can check the current speed movement, charge level or distance covered. The display is protected from external influences. factors according to the IPX7 standard and is not afraid of rain and dust.

Comfortable saddle

The HIMO C26 electric bike is equipped with a bicycle saddle from the famous brand Selle royal. The 3D gel structure of the saddle provides optimal resilience and high comfort while driving.
